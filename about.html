We're Building the
Programmer's Paradise

CodePath is a free learning platform designed specifically for BCA students — giving you structured paths, curated resources, and a community to grow with.

Your All-in-One
Coding Companion

CodePath is a free web platform built by BCA students, for BCA students. We noticed that most learners spend more time hunting for resources than actually studying — so we fixed that.

Instead of scattered YouTube playlists and random PDFs, CodePath gives you a single place with structured learning paths, a curated tech blog, free downloadable templates, and a growing community — everything you need from your first "Hello World" to your final year project.

Mission & Vision
Mission

Make Quality Tech Education Free & Structured

Our mission is to remove the barriers that stop students from learning to code — whether that's money, confusion, or lack of guidance. CodePath delivers everything a BCA student needs to go from beginner to job-ready, completely free and in one place.

Vision

Every BCA Student Graduates Placement-Ready

We envision a future where no student graduates feeling unprepared for the tech industry. CodePath aims to be the go-to platform for every BCA student in India — a community-driven, constantly growing resource that evolves with the industry.
